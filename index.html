NEO-Escape: The Experience

By Jalen Smith

12/9/23

INSTRUCTIONS: Use the UP and Down Arrow keys to move V out the way of the Darknesses

NEO-City has been placed in a time crisis.

Time has stopped in the surface realm and V has some how transported himself into the In-Between, where the creatures of the dark realm.

V must race his way through the waves of dark creatures, Darknesses, to find safety and figure out what has happened to the world.
